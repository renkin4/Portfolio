First Project PewPewRobots

PewPewRobots

This game is develop with a group of six members. 5 Artist and 1 programmer. I was the only programmer in that team. This is the Final Year Project we decided to go with.

This is a Third Person, Multiplayer, Shooter game. The main objective of this game is to go around the map looking for boxes, collect it and send it back to the base. Basically we are stealing boxes in the warehouse. AI and players are going to be the obstacle that's going to obstruct us.
